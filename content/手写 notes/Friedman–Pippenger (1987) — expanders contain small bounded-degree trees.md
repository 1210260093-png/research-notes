**Theorem.** Let $T$ be a tree on $k$ vertices of maximum degree at most $d$, rooted at $r$. Let $H=(V,E)$ be a non-empty graph such that, for every $X\subseteq V(H)$ with $|X|\le 2k-2$,
$$
|N_H(X)|\ge (d+1)|X|.
$$
Let further $v\in V(H)$ be an arbitrary vertex of $H$. Then $H$ contains a copy of $T$, rooted at $v$.
